    • ABOUT US

    The OCCHC Table Tennis Club is a long running local table tennis club in downtown Ottawa.

    Our club offers structured play every Tuesday, Thursday and Sunday and welcomes players of all ages and skills levels.

    Come join us and enjoy the wonderful sport of table tennis! Our club routinely holds club events and tournaments so club meetings are always fun and exciting!

    RULES AND REGULATIONS

    There are 28 members and some temporary visitors in the CCHC Table Tennis Club. In order to protect each player's fair playing time as well as to protect his or her rights and duties , the Club makes the following decisions:

    1. Each player's playing time is 20 minutes for each round according to the time set by the timer

    (1). The 1st player in the waiting line has the right to select 1 player from among the following 3 players in line. Before the 1st player's selection is final, anyone of the 3 following players has no right to make his or her own selection ahead. However, if any 2 players among the 3 following players do want to play together, it is allowed only if the 2 players are willing to move 2 positions behind in line so that the 1st player will still be able to have 3 players to select from.

    2. The players waiting in line is in the order according to the position of his or her name tag in line.

    (2). In case the 1st player in line does not want to select any one from the following 3 players , the 1st player is not allowed to go convincing the 5th and the 4th players to switch positions. However, In order to select the player beyond the 4th player, the 1st player can give up his or her priority and allow the 2nd or more following players to go ahead.

    (3). The CCHC Table Tennis Club highly recommend and encourage the 1st player to choose the one immediately next in the queue unless they have already played together.

    The above rules and recommendations protect each player's fair and equal rights to select and to be selected when waiting in line for playing table tennis in the Club. Please see to and follow the rules with mutual respect and friendship among all the players..

    Thank you for your attention!
